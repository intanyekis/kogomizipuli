The Newlywed Couple's Spectacular Hilltop Mansion

The 11,649-square-foot home is situated on a 1.6-acre hilltop in The Oaks, a gated enclave with extensive security measures. According to the Coldwell Banker listing, the modern-day Mediterranean single-story house was built in 2008 and features a first-class crystal chandelier, wall-mounted candelabras, high ceilings, fountains, a tiled pool, hand-carved decoration, accents, a luxurious kitchen, a cinema house, a gym, a game room, multiple waterfalls, a guest house, winery, a wash basin for pets and 9 huge bathrooms.

The honeymoon suite contains a fireplace in the sitting area, twin stunning walk-in closets, a gym, a stunning bathroom with a large soaking tub and steam shower, and a private balcony. The main bedroom's dressing area is enormous, with enough room for Britney's wardrobe, which has lately been revealed to have quite a collection of clothing. The suite features include a 10-seat movie theater, wine cellar, piano bar area, and wood-paneled office room. Spears' bedroom features a fireplace and a dramatic arch entrance in natural tones of white, beige, and grey. Next to the primary bedroom is a cozy seating area with a crystal chandelier, creating a magnificent yet welcoming ambiance. To receive their closest friends or her children, with whom she has a close relationship, they chose a dining room that is spacious and bright. It is so classy but high technology as well.

Inside Britney And Sam’s New Love Nest

Meanwhile, the enormous expensive kitchen next to the living room has a walk-in pantry, eating area, and breakfast bar. There are also pleasant outdoor cooking and sitting areas with glimpses of the property's landscaped gardens. The kitchen is equipped with cutting-edge appliances, a breakfast bar, and a central island where everything they need will be.

The stunning Calabasas property also has luxurious furnishings and opulent facilities, which we expected from Spears. The best attraction is the 10-meter-high (33-foot) ceiling foyer in the main living area. It is embellished with exquisite cut stones. Following that are wide-arched corridors with custom-made chandeliers throughout the home. Matching candelabras and hand-carved woods adorn the abode's mounting walls. Many wooden furniture were installed which features beautiful finishes such as mahogany, cedar, and oak.

The property also boasts its own heated spa with his-and-hers getting dressed spaces in the main suite and a sunken bathtub, double vanity, and powder area in each bathroom. The bathroom offers a relaxing atmosphere wherein the lovers enjoy the guests who will come to their house soon.

The Magnificent Outdoors

Meanwhile, a magnificent fountain stands outside, overlooking the mosaic-tiled pools. The newlyweds can also enjoy a sizeable oval jacuzzi, various cascading waterfalls, water slides, and an outdoor Grill pavilion for a fun day under the sun. A 55-foot pool with a slide and a Jacuzzi, as well as a fountain with a cascade, are located outside. The view of nature plus the luxurious facilities gives the couple a cozy vibe within their home.

The Singer Britney Spear is known for her passion for stunning supercars. That is why she can keep them safe in the four available parking spaces. According to the official ad, the garages include built-in storage, a brick, and gated driveway, a golf cart, and an expansive driveway. There is also a five-car parking garage. A separate one-bedroom, a one-bathroom guesthouse with its own kitchen is part of the complex.
