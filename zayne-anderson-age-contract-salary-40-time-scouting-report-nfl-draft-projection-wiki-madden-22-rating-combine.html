Zayne Anderson is an American football defensive back for the Kansas City Chiefs of the National Football League (NFL). He was born on January 3, 1997.

Anderson played in 50 games at BYU despite redshirting with injuries for 2 seasons, compiling 162 career tackles (105 solos). Anderson has the size and speed to play and played some positions and has been compared to former BYU star and Kansas City Chief, Daniel Sorensen.

Anderson was signed and as an undrafted free agent by the Kansas City Chiefs after going unselected in the 2021 NFL Draft. After being released with final cuts on August 31, 2021. Anderson was signed to the teams practice squad and was promoted to the active roster on December 14, 2021.
