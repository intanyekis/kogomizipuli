Popularly known as The Rock in World Wrestling Entertainment (WWE), Dwayne Johnson is an American actor and professional wrestler with an estimated net worth of $320 million.

Dwayne Douglas Johnson in real life, The Rock was born on May 2, 1972, in Hayward, Alameda, California. He played college football at the University of Miami, where he helped the team to a national championship in 1991. When he debuted on the ring in 1996, he sustained a successful career in wrestling. On his 17th year as a professional wrestler, he became the WWE Champion after the most awaited and action-packed event in WrestleMania XXVII.

Two years after joining the WWF, Dwayne Johnson had tasted success after winning the championship. Throughout his career, he has won 17 championships, which include two WWF Intercontinental Championships, five WWF Tag Team Championships, and 10 world heavyweight championships. He also won the Royal Rumble in 2000.

Apart from being a wrestler, Dwayne Johnson is also known to be an actor. He is most popular for his appearance in film projects like The Rundown, Walking Tall, Be Cool, Gridiron Gang,  Get Smart, The Game Plan, Race to Witch Mountain, Tooth Fairy, Planet 51, Doom,  Faster, The Other Guys, Fast Five, Fast Six and The Scorpion King. Ranked as one of the highest paid actors, he made it to the Time 100 Most Influential People in the World list in both 2016 and 2019.
