6 Who Was Albert Dyrlund?

Albert Dyrlund was a 22-year-old comedy YouTuber who originated from Denmark.

A big part of his humor was that he heavily resembled the entertainment icon Joe Exotic during the pandemic. He cut his hair and dyed it in a way that made him look like a younger version of Joe.

Albert had a young fan base but loved to appeal to everybody. Family members of all ages could watch his videos and enjoy them.

He had over 170,000 subscribers on YouTube and 200,000 subscribers on Twitter around the time of his death.

5 How Did Albert Dyrlund Pass Away?

Albert was filming a video for his YouTube channel when he fell to his ultimate demise.

He was hanging out on a balcony when it collapsed, causing him to fall over 600 feet. Helicopters and other medics arrived on the scene but it wasn't enough to save him.

His mother announced his passing to multiple media outlets, and wished for privacy. His death resulted in thousands of people mourning him on Twitter and Instagram. Internet celebrity KSI spoke about his passing on his stream and asked his fans to pray for him.

His death was also compared to Kobe Bryant, who died a year earlier in a freak accident.

4 How Did Albert Dyrlund Become Famous?

Albert Dyrlund was a multi-talented entertainer. He made songs that went viral for their comedic content. He made comedy sketches for all sorts of platforms, primarily YouTube and TikTok.

He dabbled in prank videos. He made videos where people pretended to cheat on their loved ones. He had a prank where he had his dad get angry at his friends.

He also made challenge videos, such as the time he stayed in a cinema for 24 hours. He would do eating challenges with his father, including a challenge where they had to eat ten cheeseburgers in one sitting.

When he wasn't busy filming challenge and prank videos, Albert would make videos about traveling. He loved showing his fans his exciting lifestyle.

Albert started his YouTube channel in 2016, and in five years racked up hundreds of millions of views.

2 Was Albert Dyrlund A Famous Actor?

While Albert never appeared ambitious to have a music career, he did star in a movie called Team Albert. The movie was roughly based on him and his life. It was a fictional comedy made specifically for families and younger audiences.

1 Everything To Know About Albert Dyrlund's Music Career

Albert based a lot of his success on his aspiring music career. He used his musical talents as a platform for his comedy, making hilarious song after hilarious song. These pieces raked in millions of views on YouTube. Albert's most successful song, "Hellerup-dreng", garnered over six million views. And that number continues to grow today.

On many of occasions, Albert hinted on social media that he wanted to eventually make more serious music. He wanted to follow the route of Joji, and his incredible career. Unfortunately, Albert never got to see these dreams realized.
