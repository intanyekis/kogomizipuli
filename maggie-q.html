Maggie Denise Quigley, better known as Maggie Q, is a model and actress from the United States. She is most recognized for her roles in Mission: Impossible III and Live Free or Die Hard. She is also a professional model, having worked for a number of well-known modeling agencies and magazines.

Early Childhood and Education

Margaret Denise Quigley was born on May 22, 1979, in Honolulu, Hawaii, USA. Maggie is of Irish, Polish, and Vietnamese ancestry and holds American citizenship. She grew up in Honolulu alongside Andrea Quigley, Maria Quigley, and Nicole Quigley.

Maggie longed to be a model since she was a child, and she eventually made it to Hollywood. Her parents’ encouragement opened the door for her to realize her dream.
