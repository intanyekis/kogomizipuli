Who is Allie Kay?

Allie Kay or Allie Minati is a 25-year-old model and fashionista, who presumably owns the fashion label Minati. She appears to be a very private person, who deactivated her Instagram and Twitter profiles after the birth of her son with Roddy. 

Rapper Roddy Ricch, 22, has always been very private about his personal life, though fan accounts of Allie Kay show that the duo had been together for a long time. In April 2020, Allie posted a photo of Roddy with their newborn son which made the public aware of his fatherhood.

However, she deactivated her Instagram account soon after, as she reportedly felt upset with some female fans of Roddy, who expressed their disappointment that Roddy chose Allie over them. The photo of Roddy and the baby, originally posted by Allie, was circulated later by other fan accounts. 

Another Instagram account believed to belong to Allie, shares a few posts of her modeling. It also has stories and highlights of her son and her happy relationship with Roddy.

However, there have been no posts on the profile in the past 37 weeks. 

Allie's fan account with the handle @allieminatixsamaria has addressed a few common queries from her fanbase. They highlight that Allie is a person who loves privacy and is very peaceful by nature. 

Was Roddy Ricch arrested for assaulting Allie?

On August 11, 2019, the rapper was arrested from his Los Angeles home on charges of felony domestic violence. The alleged assault took place after a verbal argument with his girlfriend, that resulted in a physical altercation, reported TMZ.

However, it was not confirmed whether the girlfriend in question was Allie or not. Roddy was later released on a $50,000 bond and the charges were dropped the following year citing insufficient evidence. 

The aforementioned fan account of Allie revealed that Roddy and his baby mamma Allie have had their ups and downs but are still going strong.
