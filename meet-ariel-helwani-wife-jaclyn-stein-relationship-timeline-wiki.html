Meet Ariel Helwani Wife, Jaclyn Stein: Relationship Timeline &amp; Wiki

 Posted on June 28, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  234 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Ariel Helwani, known for his work in mixed martial arts, is married to Jaclyn Stein. The couple first met in 1994 and tied the knot in 2008. They have three children together – two girls and a boy. Jaclyn, who was born to business owners and had early exposure to the business world, has become prominent thanks to her marriage to Ariel.

Ariel, born on July 8, 1982, is a Canadian-American sports writer who entered the MMA scene after working as an intern for NBC and HBO. His career includes work as an MMA Insider for Fox Sports and a podcast host, as well as joining and later departing from ESPN.

Jaclyn, born on March 28, 1982, in Montreal, Canada, pursued a degree in business from McGill University and also studied jewelry design at the Fashion Institute of Technology in New York. She began her career as a jewelry designer at Anzie Jewelry and eventually became the CEO of the company, which was founded by her mother.

Jaclyn’s father, Barry D. Stein, is the President and CEO of Colorectal Cancer Canada and has had a successful legal career, establishing his own legal practice in 2021.

Jaclyn and Ariel’s relationship began when they met in grade 7 in Montreal in 1994. They started dating and eventually got married in 2008, building a family together with their three children.
