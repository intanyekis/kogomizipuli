Matt McCall U Mass Coach Salary & Net Worth Explored

According to the contract, Matt McCall has a base salary of about $250,000 in a year, and his net worth is estimated to be $3.5 million, but the exact details are yet to be revelead.

Matt signed the contract of being the head coach for men's basketball of UMass on March 29, 2017, and he enters his fifth season in 2021-22. According to the contract he has signed with UMass he will get the base salary of $250,000 along with the bonus of $200,000 every year with increment every year.

McCall went to the University of Florida and graduated with a degree in exercise and sports science in 2004. Similarly, he also did his Master's degree in the same University and graduated in May 2006.

Matt was initially working as the head coach for Chattanooga before getting appointed as the head coach for the Umass men's basketball.

Why Was Matt McCall Fired From U Mass?

Matt McCall seems to be fired from the Umass due to unfavorable results by the team as announced by the UMass on Tuesday. The UMass athletic director Ryan Bamford said that he has done his best as the head coach and worked to build a championship program.

But he couldn't meet the desire required by the UMass on the court, and Ryan said that the change at the end of the season will be the best for the basketball team.

McCall has been working as the UMass head coach since 2017, and during his coaching, UMass had played the best basketball of the season, with wins in four of its final six games of the regular season.
