Alodia Gosiengfiao, a cosplayer from the Philippines and co-owner of Tier One Entertainment, is well known for her online game streaming and gaming collection.

She also co-hosts the prank show Laugh Out Loud on ABS-CBN and is one of the “Ani-mates,” a group of celebrities who advertise Animax Asia. In addition, DOS Magazine named her one of the Most Influential Women in the Philippines.

The vlogger is currently trending on social media as a result of the release of her engagement video with her non-actor spouse, Christopher Quimbo.

Wil Dasovich, an ex-boyfriend of Alodia Gosiengfiao
