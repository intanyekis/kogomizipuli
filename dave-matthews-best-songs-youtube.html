10 Of Dave Matthews Best Songs (According To YouTube Views)

 Posted on June 13, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  947 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Dave Matthews Band has been making music since 1991 and they have gained quite a following since their debut. Many are obsessed with Matthews himself as his ability to play the guitar is unparalleled. Fans often listen to their songs on YouTube, as well as attend his concerts to see them in action.

This band's videos have garnered millions of views on YouTube, which comes as no surprise as they have been around for quite a while. Fans might find themselves surprised by the ranking which is based on views, as their favorite songs didn't make the list. Keep reading to learn about the ranking of Dave Matthews Band's songs according to YouTube views!

10 Stay (3.2 Million)

This song was released on their album called Before These Crowded Streets which was released back in 1988. It has been viewed over 3.2 million times on YouTube and fans love the upbeat vibe it gives off between the lyrics and numerous instruments. The music video follows Matthews and a community as they have fun on a typical summer day with plenty of music and dancing.

9 Ants Marching (3.6 Million)

The year 1995 gave this song to listeners and it was from their debut album called Under the Table and Dreaming. It was an instant hit and still remains one of his most recognizable songs to this day. The video features several flashbacks as well as closeups of the various instruments that are used throughout the song which helped it reach 3.6 million views on YouTube.

8 So Much To Say (4.1 Million)

The album called Crash brought this song to listeners' ears back in 1996 and it even won a Grammy for its success. The music video has been seen over 4.1 million times on YouTube and it follows Matthews as he is trapped in an insane asylum. It has a groovy beat that will have everyone up and on their feet as their sound is infectious.

7 Dreamgirl (4.5 Million)

This song is a bit newer as it was released in 2005 on their album called Stand Up. Matthews wrote this song himself with the help of his producer named Mark Batson and it has been viewed over 4.5 million times on YouTube.

The music video features Julia Roberts who has been friends with this singer for years. Matthews has a gritty sound to his voice in this song which gives it character as fans relate to the lyrics that he has come up with.

6 Satellite (4.9 Million)

This is another song from their debut album called Under the Table and Dreaming and the video has now been seen over 4.9 million times on YouTube. The guitar solo by Matthews is what made it so special as he came up with the technique himself after altering a finger exercise he regularly performs. Those who watch the music video will even recognize the appearance of Johnny Galecki who played Leonard Hofstadter in the show called The Big Bang Theory.

5 Grace Is Gone (6.6 Million)

Matthews wrote this song after the passing of his stepfather and fans feel the emotion in his words. It was included on his album called Busted Stuff after the original recordings from something called The Lillywhite Sessions were leaked on the internet. It has been viewed over 6.6 million times on YouTube as fans interpret the lyrics to fit their own sad situations.

4 Where Are You Going (8.4 Million)

This is another song from his album called Busted Stuff that was released back in 2002. Unlike Grace is Gone, this was an original song that was not leaked from The Lillywhite Sessions before being added to this album.

Fans have listened to this classic over 8.4 million times on YouTube and it was featured in the film called Mr. Deeds. The music video has several scenes from the film and takes viewers on a journey through several landscapes.

3 Crush (9.4 Million)

The original version of this song is over eight minutes long, but for the radio and this music video, it was cut down significantly. It has a smooth tone that one would almost expect to hear while eating at a fancy restaurant. The video has been viewed over 9.4 million times on YouTube and it was another single from their album called Before These Crowded Streets.

2 You & Me (9.4 Million)

This song came to fans with the release of their album called Big Whiskey & the GrooGrux King back in 2009. Matthews wrote this song himself while spending some time in New York and fans love the sweet sentiment this song gives.

It has been viewed over 9.4 million times on YouTube and it is still a popular song to play at weddings to this day. The music video features a variety of people sitting in chairs playing guitars and other various instruments alongside this singer as they all join together with a common love for music.

1 Crash Into Me (20 Million)

Ther song at the top of our list is none other than Crash into Me, which was released on an album called Crash back in 1996. It was nominated for a Grammy in 1998 and it has been viewed over 20 million times on YouTube since its release. The music video is fairly unique as women dance behind Matthews in long dresses with some different filtering effects that give it a strange feel compared to some of his other videos.
