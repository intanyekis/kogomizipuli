Who plays Paulo in The Night Agent? (Greyston Holt age, height, etc.)

 Posted on August 9, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  320 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Get to know Greyston Holt, the actor who plays Maddie Redfield’s (Sarah Desjardins) art professor Paolo in The Night Agent. Where have you seen Holt before?

If you’ve watched The Night Agent already, then you know who Paolo is and his importance to the plot, but we won’t spoil too much here. I’ll just say that Canadian actor Greyston Holt plays Professor Paolo, a teacher at Maddie’s college who starts a romantic relationship with her. But are Paolo’s motives as innocent as he claims? Or is there something more sinister lurking beneath his handsome exterior?

Learn more about the actor below, including where to follow him on Instagram, his age, and some of his other roles!

This handsome Canadian actor was born on September 30, 1985, in Calgary and is currently 37 years old.

Holt is a tall guy at 6 feet 2 inches. He has green eyes and medium-length brown hair. He often plays characters between the ages of 20 and 30.

The majority of Holt’s Instagram feed is comprised of stunning photographs from different landscapes and locations. He posts more photos of his surroundings than of himself and has a very curated and aesthetically pleasing feed. You can follow him @greystonholt.

Holt is best known for playing the werewolf Clayton Danvers on the popular Space television series Bitten co-starring Laura Vandervoot. But he’s been acting since 2004 and has appeared in dozens of shows and films, including Smallville, Chesapeake Shores, Durham County, Alcatraz, Fringe, Riverdale, Once Upon a Time, and more. He also recently starred opposite Mena Suvari in the Lifetime movie House of Chains.

Outside of his acting career, Holt is a musician who plays guitar and was even part of a few bands as a teenager. He also enjoys snowboarding.

See Greyston Holt in action as Paolo in The Night Agent, now streaming on Netflix.
