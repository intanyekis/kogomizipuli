Reginald Hudlin Biography and Wiki

Reginald Hudlin is an American film screenwriter, director and producer, and comic book writer who was born in Centreville, Illinois as Reginald Alan Hudlin. Alongside his older brother Warrington Hudlin, he is known as one of the Hudlin Brothers. Hudlin was President of Entertainment for Black Entertainment Television (BET) from 2005 to 2008.

Also, he has written numerous graphic novels. He co-produced the 88th Academy Awards ceremony in the year 2016 and other TV specials. Hudlin’s breakout film was the 1990s House Party, and he served as a producer on Quentin Tarantino’s 2012 film, Django Unchained, which earned him an Academy Award nomination for Best Picture. Also, he directed the 1992 film Boomerang.
